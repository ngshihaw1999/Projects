About Us

A family-run company called Chollerton Tearooms is situated in the charming hamlet of Chollerton, North Tyne Valley. We provide a cosy, welcoming setting where you may unwind with a cup of tea or coffee and handmade pastries, scones, and sandwiches.

We provide bed and breakfast lodging in addition to our tearooms in our exquisitely renovated 18th-century cottage. Our accommodations are pleasant and well-equipped, with all the comforts you could want.
